Get to know Princess Ariel

Princess Ariel is the protagonist of Disney's animated film, The Little Mermaid. She is the youngest daughter of King Triton and Queen Athena, rulers of the undersea kingdom of Atlantica. Ariel lived through much of her young life with admiration of the human world and longed to someday experience life on the surface.
